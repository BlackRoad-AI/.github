### Getting Help

#### For Users

1. **Check existing issues** - Your question may already be answered
2. **Search discussions** - Community Q&A and announcements
3. **Read the docs** - Most questions are answered in documentation

#### For Developers

1. **Architecture first** - Understand the routing philosophy
2. **Check workflow templates** - Standard CI/CD patterns
3. **Review examples** - Working code in each repository

### Asking Questions

When asking for help, include:

- **What you're trying to do** (goal, not just symptoms)
- **What you've tried** (show your work)
- **Error messages** (full stack traces)
- **Environment** (node, Python version, etc.)
